# Comprehensive Cell Marker Reference for Hematology

## Hematopoietic Stem Cells (HSCs)
CD34, KIT (CD117), THY1 (CD90), FLT3 (CD135), HOXA9, MEIS1, HLF, CRHBP

## Megakaryocyte Lineage

### MK Progenitors
CD34+, ITGA2B (CD41), GP1BA (CD42b), CD38+, MPL

### Endomitotic MKs
ITGA2B, GP1BA, PF4, PPBP, TUBB1, MYH10low, polyploid

### Platelet-Generating MKs
ITGA2B, VWF, F2R, GP9, SELP, proplatelet extensions

### HSC Niche-Supporting MKs
THPO, IGF1, CXCL12, ANGPT1, JAG1

### Inflammatory MKs (MPN-associated)
S100A8, S100A9, S100A10, S100A11, CHI3L1, CTSS, ANXA1, MPIG6B
LGALS1, TGFB1, CD53, LSP1, MYL4, CCL4, IRF8, CYBB
CXCL8, IL6, CXCL10, LCN2, VEGFA, LIF

### Translating MKs
High ribosomal genes (RPL/RPS), EIF4A1, EIF3E

## T Cell Subsets

### CD4+ T Cells
CD3D, CD3E, CD4, IL2RA, FOXP3 (Tregs), CCR7 (naive)

### CD8+ T Cells  
CD8A, CD8B, GZMA, GZMB, PRF1

### Exhausted T Cells
PDCD1 (PD-1), CTLA4, HAVCR2 (TIM3), TIGIT, LAG3, CD160

## B Cells
MS4A1 (CD20), PAX5, CD19, CD79A/B

## Myeloid Cells
LYZ, FCN1, CD14, S100A8/9 (monocytes)
TYROBP, ADA2, CD53, ITGB2, SPI1

## MPN Disease Markers

### Driver Mutations
JAK2, CALR, MPL

### Additional Mutations
ASXL1, TET2, DNMT3A, EZH2, IDH1/2, TP53, PPM1D, SRSF2

### Fibrosis Score Genes (Psaila 2020)
TGFB1, IL12A, IL1B, RAB37, TIMP1, APIP, PF4V1, VEGFA
FBLN2, SFRP1, COL6A2, COL4A2, COL5A1, PDGFRB, LOXL2, RUNX2

### Pyruvate Kinase (Metabolism)
PKM, PKLR
